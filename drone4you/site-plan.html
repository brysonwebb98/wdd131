Content

Home page

The home page will have a large image of a photo taken by a drone. It will show people who we are and what we do. It will show them different types of drone we that we have as well. There will be a section to put your information for us to reach out to you and consult.

Gallery

Gallery page will have different photos of drone pictures in different locations. When you put in a specific location it will pull up images from there.
